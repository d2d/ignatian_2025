Xavier - Castle of Saint Francis

May 29, 2025

We travel past Pamplona, where Ignatius was grievously wounded in 1521 during the siege by French forces. Imagining the rugged terrain and his difficult transport back to Loyola Castle after such a devastating injury, we continue our journey to the ancestral castle of Saint Francis Xavier, Ignatius's first companion and the great missionary to the East. We spend the night here, reflecting after some hikes and a nice meal.

Rome - Eternal City

June 2-4, 2025

After an early morning flight, our pilgrimage culminates in Rome, where Ignatius founded the Society of Jesus and died. We visit Paul Outside the Walls, walk the ancient Appia Antica, pray at the Gesu, San Ignazio, and Saint Mary Major. We visit the Jesuit Refugee Service at the Jesuit Curia, witnessing the Society's ongoing mission of service, and then are treated a view of the city and the Vatican from the roof of the Curia. We conclude with Mass in the rooms where Saint Ignatius died, next to the Gesu.
